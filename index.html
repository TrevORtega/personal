Introduction

Trevor Ortega is a Software Developer and Computer Vision Researcher who was born in New Mexico, raised in Arizona, and attending University in Bellingham, Washington. Trevor became interested in Computer Science in High School and has learned to hone his skills for building software through multiple internships (i.e. Jethro Mobile LLC and the Faithlife Corporation) as well as University courses at Western Washington University. Trevor also gained an passion in Machine Learning and Computer Vision after working on multiple research projects under the advisement of WWU professor, Scott Wehrwein.
